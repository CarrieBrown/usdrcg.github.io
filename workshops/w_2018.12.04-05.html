XSEDE HPC Workshop: Big Data

December 4-5, 2018 at the University of South Dakota, Patterson Hall 225

XSEDE along with the Pittsburgh Supercomputing Center is pleased to announce a two day Big Data workshop, to be held December 4-5, 2018. In addition to Hadoop and Spark, this workshop includes training in machine learning using Recommender System with Spark and deep learning with Tensorflow.

  • When: December 4th & 5th, 10AM - 4PM Central Time both days
  • Where: Patterson Hall, room 225 (map)
  • Cost: FREE! (registration required)
