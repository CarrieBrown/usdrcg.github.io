Welcome to USD's Research Computing Group

A range of disciplines at USD utilize the high performance computing cluster to streamline research tasks. We provide support for faculty, staff, students, and partners for data-driven and computational research profects.

We offer new user training via the Welcome Wagon and many resources to researchers.

USD HPC staff is here to help integrate this technology into your research and into your classroom.
